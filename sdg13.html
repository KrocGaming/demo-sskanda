SDG 13 Solutions

Horizon17 is dedicated to addressing SDG 13: Climate Action. We offer comprehensive solutions that enable businesses to reduce their carbon footprint, mitigate climate change risks, and transition to a net-zero economy. Our services include carbon assessment, auditing, consulting, and offsetting, all powered by advanced technology.

Carbon Assessment, Audit, Consulting & Offsetting Service (Horizon17 CCAE)

We provide an end-to-end carbon offset platform that includes carbon credit assessment and exchange.

Our services help businesses achieve their climate ambitions and progress toward a net-zero carbon and climate-resilient global economy.

We leverage a blockchain-based framework for immutability, traceability, and integrity.

Our platform uses AI for data acquisition, collation, and analysis.

AI-Based Carbon Footprint Assessment and Reporting Solution

Comprehensive accounting of Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions.

AI engine to extract, transform, and validate inputs with attributable, pseudo-attributable, and non-attributable carbon emissions.

Reports are also generated for individual carbon footprints.

Blockchain-Based Carbon Credit Exchange

Platform for buyers and sellers of Carbon Credit Certificates (CCCs).

Transparent and automated trades using Smart Contracts.

Providing traceability of CCCs throughout their lifecycle.

Impact

  • • Reduce greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions.
  • • Facilitate carbon offsetting and carbon trading.
  • • Promote climate action and support the transition to a net-zero economy.

We are a technology-driven company specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain Technology, Internet of Things (IoT), and Renewable Energy Systems.

Our focus is on developing innovative, efficient, and sustainable solutions that drive progress across industries.
