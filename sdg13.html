SDG 13 Solutions

SDG 13 calls for urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. With 140 countries committing to net-zero targets by 2050—covering 90% of global emissions—the challenge remains immense, as many nations, including India, struggle to meet these goals. Horizon17 addresses this through innovative carbon management solutions, helping businesses and individuals transition to a net-zero economy.

"As of 2022, 25% of countries are unlikely to achieve net-zero by 2050, underscoring the urgent need for scalable carbon offset solutions."

Our Solution: Carbon Assessment, Audit, Consulting & Offsetting Service (Horizon17 CCAE)

Horizon17 CCAE is an end-to-end platform that empowers organizations to assess, audit, consult, and offset their carbon emissions using advanced technology.

Products

AI-Based Carbon Footprint Assessment and Reporting (AI-CFAR)

  • Comprehensive accounting of Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions.
  • AI extracts, transforms, and validates data, categorizing emissions as attributable, pseudo-attributable, and non-attributable.
  • Generates detailed reports for organizations and individual carbon footprints.

Blockchain-Based Carbon Credit Exchange (BCCE)

  • A transparent platform for trading Carbon Credit Certificates (CCCs) using smart contracts.
  • Ensures traceability and integrity throughout the CCC lifecycle.
  • Enhances liquidity in the $1 trillion carbon market, projected to reach $2.68 trillion by 2028.

How It Works

  • AI leverages deep web searches and document analysis to validate emissions data.
  • Blockchain ensures immutable, traceable carbon credit transactions.
  • Consulting services guide businesses toward net-zero strategies.

"Compliance" Carbon Offset Programs

  • Clean Development Mechanism (CDM).
  • California Compliance Offset Program
  • Joint Implementation (JI)
  • Regional Greenhouse Gas Intiative (RGGI)
  • Alberta Emission Offset Program (AEOP)

Compliance markets are created and regulated by mandatory national,regional or international carbon reduction regimes.Compliance programs exist as regional or national cap-and-trade emission trading schemes

"Voluntary" Carbon Offset Programs

  • American Carbon Registry
  • Climate Action Reserve (CAR)
  • The Gold Standard
  • Plan Vivo
  • The Verified Carbon Standard (VCS)

Voluntary markets function outside of Compliance markets and enable companies and individuals to purchase to purchase carbon offsets on a Voluntary basis with no intended use for complaince purposes

Impact

Horizon17 CCAE reduces greenhouse gas emissions, facilitates carbon trading, and supports climate resilience, directly contributing to SDG 13’s goals of emission reduction and climate action.
