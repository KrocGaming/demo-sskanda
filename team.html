Leadership

Dr. Milan Patnaik

Founder & CEO

Colonel (Dr) Milan Patnaik is a doctorate from IIT Madras and completed his Masters in Cybersecurity as the Topper from IIT Madras as well as MUNI Czech Republic. He has over 24 years of experience in developing cutting-edge technologies as part of Army's technology vertical and as an advisor to multiple IITs and foreign universities. His research spans offensive security, malware evasion, and AI-based blockchain solutions. He commanded the Army's largest telecommunication unit and has led enterprise-level organizations with 1000+ members across multiple verticals with a focus on national infrastructure security, including defense and telecommunication sectors. He has mentored over 40 startups, fostering "Made in India" innovations that focus on creating indigenous solutions to address critical challenges.

Rumjhum Gupta

Founder & Chief Sustainability Officer

Rumjhum Gupta, an MIT-certified Chief Sustainability Officer and MSc from London School of Economics, founded Horizon17 Technologies and Sustainability Pvt. Ltd. with a vision to provide technology-driven sustainability solutions aligned with the UN's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). With over 20 years of global experience, including roles as Nestlé's Global Corporate Spokesperson, founder of EcoMedia Solutions, and co-founder of UHeaIT Solutions Inc, Rumjhum brings deep expertise in corporate sustainability and communications to drive ESG excellence. Partnering with former Indian military experts in sustainability, technology, operations, and IT, she leads the company in delivering accredited sustainability solutions through comprehensive SDG audits and assessments.

Colonel Pankaj Awasthi

Founder & CEO

Colonel Pankaj Awasthi is a military veteran with 22 years of experience, specializing in strategic leadership, technology management, and cybersecurity. He holds an MTech (Gold Medalist) in Computer Science, an MBA in IT & Systems Management, an MSc in Strategic Studies, and is a CISSP certified professional. As Security Tower Head at Unique Identification Authority of India (Aadhaar), he secures the world's largest PII repository for over 1.4 billion individuals. Previously, as Principal Cloud Consultant at Hewlett Packard Enterprise, he led innovative tech projects and mentored young professionals. He managed a 1000+ technologist unit overseeing IT and cybersecurity across 1200 Sq Km, represented India in an international staff course in Indonesia, and worked with the Indian Embassy in Jakarta.

Colonel Rajesh Choudhary

Co-Founder & COO

Colonel Rajesh Choudhary is a military veteran with 21 years of experience, holding an MBA from Indian School of Business and an MSc in Strategic Studies. He brings expertise in ESG, operations management, public policy, and fostering sustainable organizational growth. He has excelled in strategic implementation, cross-departmental coordination, and operational efficiency. He has led projects for state and central governments, ensuring alignment with national priorities and sustainability. His expertise includes procurement, resource management, risk mitigation, and stakeholder engagement to drive business development. Recognized with two gallantry awards for his military operations, he has also spearheaded social impact initiatives and promoted ethical governance, enhancing public perception of military contributions.
